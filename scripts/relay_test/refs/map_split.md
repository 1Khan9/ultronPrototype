# Valorant — Map: SPLIT
## Complete Callout Reference (corpus generation ground truth)

**Map type:** Two-site, three-lane, defender-sided  
**Setting:** Tokyo, Japan — a fictional near-future city with Asian urban architecture  
**Signature mechanic:** Rope ascenders (silent vertical traversal, two-way; vulnerable during use; audible to nearby enemies)  
**Current competitive pool:** Yes (as of June 2026, Season V26 Act 3)  
**Attack/defense win rates:** ~48% attack / ~52–53% defense

---

## MAP OVERVIEW — LAYOUT STRUCTURE

Split is a **three-lane map**:
- **Left lane → A Site** (accessed via A Main and A Ramps/A Lobby)
- **Right lane → B Site** (accessed via B Main / Garage)
- **Mid corridor** (Top Mid / Bottom Mid), connecting both sites via Mail and Vents; heavily contested

**Vertical identity:** Two levels throughout. Heaven/Rafters positions dominate both sites. Rope ascenders (not teleporters) allow movement between floors at three key locations.

**Rope ascender locations (3 total):**
1. A Sewer → A Lobby (two ropes side by side)
2. Mid Vent → A Tower / A Heaven
3. B Hell → B Tower / B Heaven

## KEY STRATEGIC CONCEPTS — SPLIT

### Mid Control Priority
- Mid is the most contested area each round
- Controlling **Top Mid** gives defenders safety and rotational speed
- Attackers winning **Bottom Mid + Vents** opens A Tower flank or B Link access
- **Mail** is a common early defender position for mid info

### The Rope Ascenders — Tactical Notes
- **A Sewer ropes:** Two side-by-side ropes. Attackers use to cross from A Lobby to A Sewer toward Mid. Can be used to drop from A Lobby level back down to Sewer.
- **Mid Vent rope:** Single rope ascending from Bottom Mid Vent area to A Tower height. Key for attacker mid-to-A rotations. Defenders can drop down from A Tower to contest.
- **B Hell rope:** Single rope ascending from B Hell ground level to B Tower / B Heaven. Defenders use to quickly get to B Tower from CT level. Very exposed while ascending.

### Heaven Control = Site Control
- Both sites are dominated by their Heaven positions
- **A Heaven (A Tower):** Requires clearing via Viper Screen, smokes, or flashing before site entry; otherwise defenders will pick off attackers on Ramps and Main
- **B Heaven (B Tower):** Must be controlled before crossing Garage; attackers flanked from above die instantly

### Attacking Approaches
- **A Execute:** Full push through A Main + Ramps simultaneously; require smokes on Screens + Heaven; flash into site
- **B Execute:** Full push through Garage; require smokes on B Heaven + Alley; use mollies on B default boxes
- **Mid-to-A:** Take Bottom Mid, go through Vent, exit A Tower; flank defenders holding A from behind
- **Mid-to-B:** Take Top Mid, go through Mail/Market toward B Link into B Lobby; split B Site from two angles

### Common Plant Spots
- **A Default:** Behind Terminal (center holo-box); covered from A Heaven and Back Site
- **A Back Site:** Rear wall area near A Rafters; well-covered but harder CT execute
- **B Default:** Center of B Site near boxes; covered from B Heaven and Back Pillar
- **B Long Plant / Open Plant:** Further plant position on B Site; exposes post-plant team to retake angles from CT
