## 3. ABILITY QUICK-REFERENCE (Useful for relay commentary)

### Duelists

**Jett**
- **Cloudburst (C):** Throws a brief smoke cloud that curves; blocks vision temporarily.
- **Updraft (Q):** Launches Jett upward; enables high-angle play.
- **Tailwind (E):** Dashes in movement direction; repositions or escapes. Resets on kill.
- **Blade Storm (X):** Summons storm of throwing knives; primary fire throws one (precise, not spread), alt-fire throws all. Reloads on kill. One-shots headshot.

**Phoenix**
- **Blaze (C):** Curved fire wall; blocks vision, heals Phoenix if he stands in it, damages enemies.
- **Curveball (Q):** Flashing orb that curves left or right around corners; cannot self-blind (Phoenix is immune).
- **Hot Hands (E):** Firebomb; damages enemies in zone, heals Phoenix in zone.
- **Run It Back (X):** Phoenix marks spawn point, dies or timer expires = revived at mark with full HP. Any kills during ult are eliminated.

**Raze**
- **Boom Bot (C):** Ground robot that seeks enemies along ground and explodes.
- **Blast Pack (Q):** Sticky grenade; can be detonated to fling Raze for mobility or damage enemies.
- **Paint Shells (E):** Cluster grenade that splits into bomblets on impact.
- **Showstopper (X):** Rocket launcher; one-shots on direct hit, large radius.

**Reyna**
- **Leer (C):** Throws a floating eye that Nearsights (blurs vision) all enemies who look at it.
- **Devour (Q):** Consumes soul orb from killed enemy; heals to full HP, even overheal.
- **Dismiss (E):** Consumes soul orb; brief invulnerability + phasing (can move through walls).
- **Empress (X):** Combat trance — increased fire rate, equip/reload speed, infinite Devour/Dismiss charges. Kill refreshes duration.

**Yoru**
- **FAKEOUT (C):** Deploys a decoy clone that walks forward and mimics footsteps; or deploy audio-only fake.
- **BLINDSIDE (Q):** Teleport fragment that flashes when it hits a surface (can bounce off walls).
- **GATECRASH (E):** Tether point; Yoru can teleport to it from anywhere. Can send tether ahead to fake teleport.
- **DIMENSIONAL DRIFT (X):** Enters "Ghost" dimension — invisible, can traverse map, exit flashes nearby enemies.

**Neon**  
*(Note: Nerfed in Patch 12.09 — air-sprint momentum removed, High Gear fuel drain increased)*
- **Fast Lane (C):** Fires two lines of electric walls forward, blocking vision.
- **Relay Bolt (Q):** Charged bolt that bounces off one wall and Concusses nearby enemies on impact.
- **High Gear (E):** Activates sprint (massive movement speed); at max speed can slide (long-duration low-profile move that enters crouch-stance). Fuel drains on sprint, recharges on kills.
- **Overdrive (X):** Full sprint instantly activated, unlimited fuel for duration, fires continuous lightning beams from hands.

**Iso**
- **Undercut (C):** Energy pulse that phases through walls and inflicts Fragile debuff (enemies take more damage for 4s).
- **Double Tap (Q):** Focus timer; killing an enemy generates an energy orb that, if absorbed, creates a damage-absorbing shield (one-hit).
- **Contingency (E):** Deploys a moving bullet-proof energy wall (un-destroyable) that blocks sightlines.
- **Kill Contract (X):** Pulls a targeted enemy into a 1v1 dimensional duel; winner survives.

**Waylay**
- **Saturate (C):** Throws a cluster of light that explodes on ground contact; Hinders (slows movement, fire rate, reload, recoil recovery, jump height) nearby enemies.
- **Light Speed (Q):** Double dash forward (alt-fire = single dash); first dash can go upward.
- **Refract (E):** Creates a light beacon on ground; reactivate to return to beacon at light speed while invulnerable. Recharges on 2 kills.
- **Convergent Paths (X):** Creates an afterimage that projects a forward beam; Waylay gains a speed boost and the beam expands, Hindering all players in area.

### Controllers

**Brimstone**
- **Stim Beacon (C):** Deploys beacon that buffs nearby allies with Rapidfire (increased fire rate + movement accuracy).
- **Incendiary (Q):** Launches incendiary grenade that creates fire zone, dealing damage over time.
- **Sky Smoke (E):** Uses a map interface to call in up to 3 smoke drops at chosen locations; longest-lasting smokes in game.
- **Orbital Strike (X):** Calls in a laser beam that sweeps target area, dealing heavy continuous damage.

**Viper**
- **Snake Bite (C):** Fires canister that shatters into a corrosive acid pool (Fragile debuff + damage over time).
- **Poison Cloud (Q):** Throwable emitter that creates a persistent toxin cloud when activated; toggleable fuel cost.
- **Toxic Screen (E):** Deploys a long line of emitters that form a tall toxin wall when activated; can stretch across map.
- **Viper's Pit (X):** Drops a large toxic cloud over a wide area; enemies inside are Nearsighted and Decayed; Viper is invisible if she re-enters her own pit.

**Omen**
- **Paranoia (C):** Short-range shadow bolt that Nearsights any player it hits (pierces walls).
- **Shrouded Step (Q):** Short teleport; Omen vanishes briefly then reappears at target location.
- **Dark Cover (E):** Places smokeball anywhere on the map via globe interface; can phase through walls.
- **From the Shadows (X):** Selects any location on map; Omen teleports there (visible to enemies as shadow during travel; can be cancelled to fool).

**Astra**
- **Gravity Well (C/useable star):** A placed star activated to pull enemies toward the center, then Concuss them.
- **Nova Pulse (C/useable star):** A placed star activated to release a shockwave that Concusses nearby enemies.
- **Nebula / Dissipate (Q):** Activates a placed star to create a smoke; or fake-pulses star for 1 second (brief smoke + can retrieve star).
- **Astral Form + Cosmic Divide (X):** Astral Form = global map view to place stars anywhere; Cosmic Divide = calls in a massive permanent wall across map dividing it in half (bullet- and audio-dampening).

**Harbor**
- **Cove (C):** Throws a sphere of water that forms a shield dome protecting anything inside from bullets.
- **High Tide (Q):** Sends a wave forward along the ground; can be curved by aiming; blocks vision and slows enemies.
- **Storm Surge (E):** Fires a surge of water that fans out across the map, Concussing enemies in the wave.
- **Reckoning (X):** Harbor summons a geyser storm at a location; geysers chase enemies and Concuss on impact.

**Clove**  
*(Best agent in S26 Act 3 — 53.7–54.8% win rate, ~15% Radiant pick rate)*
- **Pick-Me-Up (C / 200 creds):** On kill/damage-assist: absorbs life force; gains temporary HP overheal (up to 50 HP) + Haste (movement speed boost) for 8s.
- **Meddle (Q / 250 creds):** Throws an immortality fragment that erupts after a brief delay; Decays all enemies caught in blast (reduces max health, making them fragile).
- **Ruse (E / Signature — 1 free + 2nd charge at 150 creds):** Deploys smokes from overhead interface; smokes last ~13.5s; **can be used after death** (post-death smokes limited to area near body). 40s cooldown.
- **Not Dead Yet (X / 8 ult points):** Self-revive — Clove resurrects at death location with 2s invulnerability. Must secure a kill or assist within 10s or die again.

**Miks**  
*(Agent 30, Controller, released March 18 2026 — Patch 12.05)*  
*(First controller with healing; D/F-tier in some lists; community still learning)*
- **M-Pulse (C):** Throws a deployable synthesizer. Toggle between Concuss mode and Healing mode with ALT-FIRE before throwing. On landing, pulses either concuss or heal players in range. (Bug fixed in 12.10: healing no longer lingers briefly after exit.)
- **Harmonize (Q):** Target an ally; activates Combat Stim on both Miks and the target that refreshes on each kill. ALT-FIRE = stim self only.
- **Waveform (E / Signature):** Map targeter to set smoke locations; deploys smokes instantly at marked positions. Free charge per round.
- **Bassquake (X / 8 ult points):** Releases a massive forward wave of sonic radiance — Deafens, Slows, and Knocks Back enemies hit.

### Initiators

**Sova**
- **Owl Drone (C):** Deploys a remote drone that Sova pilots; can tag enemies (revealing them briefly) and fire a dart that Reveals tagged targets.
- **Shock Bolt (Q):** Fires a bow bolt that bounces off walls and explodes with shock damage. Can charge for more bounces (max 2 bounces).
- **Recon Bolt (E / Signature):** Fires a bouncing recon arrow that deploys a sonar emitter on contact; pulses and reveals all enemies in line of sight. Destructible.
- **Hunter's Fury (X):** Fires up to 3 wall-piercing energy blasts across the entire map length; each shot Reveals and damages any enemy hit.

**Breach**
- **Aftershock (C):** Places a charge on a wall that fires a burst of blinding energy through it (damages enemies on the other side).
- **Flashpoint (Q):** Fires a flash charge through a wall that blinds all players caught in the blast on the other side.
- **Fault Line (E / Signature):** Sends a seismic blast forward through terrain; Concusses (disrupts vision) all enemies caught in the path.
- **Rolling Thunder (X):** Sends an earthquake cascade forward across a large area; Concusses and knocks back all enemies in the zone.

**Skye**
- **Regrowth (C):** Heals nearby allies in range while Skye has charges; cannot heal Skye herself.
- **Guiding Light (Q):** Throws a hawk trinket that Skye can detonate to Flash enemies in its cone of sight. Can maneuver the hawk before detonating.
- **Trailblazer (E / Signature):** Remote-controls a Tasmanian tiger that leaps and Concusses the first enemy it hits or on manual trigger.
- **Seekers (X):** Sends three seekers that track down the three nearest enemies, briefly Nearsighting them on contact.

**KAY/O**
- **FRAG/ment (C):** Throws an explosive fragment that sticks to ground/surfaces and pulses multiple times, dealing damage in the zone.
- **FLASH/drive (Q):** Throws a flash canister that bursts and blinds enemies in range. Can be cooked (hold E to prime for a shorter pop time).
- **ZERO/point (E / Signature):** Throws a suppression blade that embeds in surfaces; pulses and Suppresses (disables abilities) all enemies caught in range. Enemies are Revealed while Suppressed.
- **NULL/cmd (X):** KAY/O overloads with radiant energy; pulses Suppression over a wide area repeatedly during ult. KAY/O can be revived by allies if killed during ult.

**Fade**
- **Prowler (C):** Releases a Prowler creature that chases enemies in a straight path; Nearsights on contact. Can be directed with crosshair.
- **Seize (Q):** Throws a ball that creates a Seize zone on landing; enemies caught are tethered to the zone and Deafened briefly.
- **Haunt (E / Signature):** Throws a creature that reveals enemies near it and marks them with a trail (revealed through walls briefly). Enemies can destroy Haunt.
- **Nightfall (X):** Sends a wave of nightmare energy across the entire map in a direction; Decays, Deafens, and marks with a trail any enemy hit.

**Gekko**
- **Mosh Pit (C):** Throws Mosh, who creates an expanding area of damage on landing. Retrievable if Mosh survives.
- **Dizzy (Q):** Launches Dizzy, who flies forward and charges a plasma bolt that Blinds enemies in line of sight. Retrievable after use.
- **Wingman (E / Signature):** Summons Wingman who runs toward the spike target (plant or defuse site); can plant/defuse the spike. Concusses enemies in area. Retrievable.
- **Thrash (X):** Controls Thrash, a larger creature that lunges at an enemy and Detains them briefly. Thrash has HP and can be killed; can destroy it manually for a wider Detain radius.

**Tejo**
- **Special Delivery (C):** Throws a sticky concussion grenade; alt-fire = single-bounce version.
- **Stealth Drone (Q):** Deploys and pilots a stealth drone; can fire a pulse that Suppresses and Reveals enemies hit.
- **Guided Salvo (E / Signature):** Uses an AR targeting system; selects up to 2 target locations; fires autonomous missiles that navigate to targets and explode on arrival.
- **Armageddon (X):** Targets an origin and end point on map; launches a wave of explosions along the strike path.

### Sentinels

**Cypher**
- **Trapwire (C):** Deployable trip wire between two walls; triggered enemies are Revealed, Slowed, and can be Stunned if not destroyed.
- **Cyber Cage (Q):** Throws a remote cage that creates a vision-blocking zone and slows enemies who walk through it.
- **Spycam (E / Signature):** Places a hidden camera Cypher can view remotely; can fire a tracking dart that Reveals the tagged enemy.
- **Neural Theft (X):** Extracts data from a dead enemy's corpse; Reveals all surviving enemies to the entire team.

**Sage**
- **Barrier Orb (C):** Creates a large solid wall of ice that can be rotated before placement.
- **Slow Orb (Q):** Throws a slow field that creates a chilling zone; enemies in it are slowed and make footstep noise.
- **Healing Orb (E / Signature):** Heals a targeted ally (or herself) for full HP over time. Single-charge per round.
- **Resurrection (X):** Resurrects a dead ally at their death location with full HP.

**Killjoy**
- **Nanoswarm (C):** Throwable grenade that goes invisible on landing; activate remotely to deploy swarm that deals high damage in area (great post-plant).
- **Alarmbot (Q):** Deploys a hidden bot that hunts enemies in range and triggers to deal damage and apply Vulnerable debuff.
- **Turret (E / Signature):** Deploys a turret that fires on enemies in its 180-degree cone. Does not kill; tracks/reveals enemy positions.
- **Lockdown (X):** Deploys a device that charges for ~13s; when active, Detains all enemies in its large radius.

**Chamber**
- **Trademark (C):** Places a trap on the ground; when an enemy enters radius, it marks and Slows them and surrounding enemies.
- **Headhunter (Q):** Equips a heavy pistol; alt-fire scopes in. Bullets cost 100 creds each (8 max). One-shots to head at any range when scoped.
- **Rendezvous (E / Signature):** Places two teleport anchors; Chamber can teleport to the anchor when on the ground and within range. Anchors can be recalled.
- **Tour De Force (X):** Summons a sniper rifle (one-shot anywhere on body); on kill, creates a Slow field around the body. 5 bullets, 7 ult cost.

**Deadlock**  
*(A-tier, 53.2% WR; metabot notes S-tier; strong sentinel post buffs)*
- **GravNet (C):** Throws or lobs a grenade that forces all caught enemies to crouch and move very slowly.
- **Sonic Sensor (Q):** Deploys a monitor that detects significant sound (footsteps, gunfire); Concusses the area when triggered. Retrievable.
- **Barrier Mesh (E / Signature):** Throws a disc that deploys anchor barriers from an origin point, blocking character movement.
- **Annihilation (X):** Fires a pulse that cocoons the first enemy hit in nanowire; the cocooned enemy is dragged to the origin if not freed by allies. Cannot be killed while cocooned — only detained.

**Vyse**  
*(B-tier by win rate; niche defensive pick; strong on defense 51.6% vs 48.5% attack)*
- **Shear (C):** Places a hidden wall trap; when an enemy crosses the trigger, an indestructible wall bursts from the ground trapping them for duration.
- **Arc Rose (Q):** Places a stealthed Arc Rose on a surface (or through it via alt-fire); reactivate to blind all players looking at it. Retrievable and redeployable.
- **Razorvine (E / Signature):** Throws a nest of liquid metal that becomes invisible on landing; activates into a large razorvine field that Slows and damages all players who move through it.
- **Steel Garden (X):** Sends out metallic thorns in a wide area — enemies in range have their primary weapon **jammed** (unusable), wrapped in metallic vines for the duration.

**Veto**  
*(Released October 7, 2025 — Patch 11.07b; C-tier / situational)*
- **Chokehold (Q):** Throws a viscous mutation fragment that deploys a trap on impact; holds caught enemies in place, Deafens and Decays them.
- **Crosscut (C):** Deploys a vortex on the ground; while in range and looking at the vortex, reactivate to teleport to it. Requires line of sight for activation.
- **Interceptor (E / Signature):** Deploys a Phage that neutralizes nearby bouncing/destructible projectiles (counters Boom Bot, Wingman, etc.). Has only 20 HP — destructible by gunfire.
- **Evolution (X / 7 ult points):** Grants Veto combat enhancement (increased fire rate + reload speed) plus total immunity to debuffs for entire round.

## 5. META NARRATIVE (V26 Act 3 — June 2026)

### The Clove-Centric Meta
Clove sits alone at the top. Every top team comp includes her. The post-death smoke mechanic fundamentally alters how controllers are valued — Clove provides full controller utility even when dead, which no other agent can match. Her Pick-Me-Up enables aggressive hybrid play that traditional controllers lack.

**Anti-Clove sentiment for relay:** "Why are you dying for a controller?" / "You gave them free stats." — Clove punishes passive teams.

### Double-Initiator Dominance
The most reliable setup in ranked and esports is 2 Initiators + 1 Controller + 1 Duelist + 1 Sentinel. Information throughput wins rounds. Teams that skip an initiator for a second duelist usually lose site executes at higher elo.

### The Chamber Comeback
Chamber was heavily nerfed in Ep 6 but slowly climbed back. His Headhunter + Tour De Force sniper fantasy remains intact, and Rendezvous escape mirrors Jett's Tailwind enough that players who don't want to learn Jett's air-strafe run him.

### Viper Map-Lock
On Breeze specifically, Viper remains near-mandatory. Her Toxic Screen can split the entire B-side of Breeze, creating a 2v5 wall. No other controller can replicate this. Breeze without Viper = contested maps where you fight every angle.

### KAY/O Situational Revival
KAY/O is only good when the enemy team is loaded with ability-reliant agents (Skye, Killjoy, Reyna). His ZERO/point suppression is devastating in the right matchup. In casual ranked where teams run hybrid comps, he's underwhelming.

### Neon Post-12.09 Nerf
The 12.09 patch (May 12, 2026) removed Neon's air-sprint momentum — she can no longer bunny-hop into a slide for explosive entry angles. This dropped her from A/S-tier to B/C-tier in several lists. High Gear fuel drain also increased. She still has the fastest movement speed in the game but requires more discipline.

### Regional Pro Meta (VCT 2026 Kickoff)
- **Americas:** Viper (54.69%), Waylay (42.71%), Astra (37.50%). Double-controller and Waylay innovation.
- **Pacific:** Yoru dominant (64.58%), Viper (46.88%), Omen (43.75%). Double-duelist chaos. Pacific loves deception.
- **EMEA:** Yoru (44.79%), Viper (43.23%), Sova (42.71%). Highest regional win rates. Structured with Fade/Viper/Yoru.
- **China:** Yoru (50.52%), Omen (44.79%), Viper (44.27%). Similar to Pacific; Yoru globally dominant in pro.

**Yoru is the pro-play meta king** even when ranked players largely avoid him (high learning curve = lower ranked WR).

## 7. MAP-BY-MAP META (V26 Act 3 Active Pool)

### ASCENT
- **Layout:** Semi-open; contested mid (B-Mid / Market / A-Mid); two sites with hawker/tree/link.
- **Attack/Defense:** Generally 50/50; mid control swings rounds.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Omen + Jett + Sova + Killjoy + KAY/O (57.3%)
- **Best Agents:** Killjoy (B-site Turret + Nanoswarm post-plant), Sova (Recon Bolt mid + A default), Jett (Operator from catwalk/tree), Omen (Dark Cover mid), KAY/O (Suppress mid defenders)
- **Key Note:** Ascent returned Patch 12.08; raw-aim and default-heavy strategies rewarded. Mid control critical.
- **Avoid:** Harbor (no water utility), Gekko (no clear spike site advantage)

### BREEZE
- **Layout:** Largest map; open sites with long sightlines; two sites connected by mid zip.
- **Attack/Defense:** Defense-sided on A (large site); contested mid hall.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Viper + Jett + Sova + Killjoy + KAY/O (57.5%)
- **Best Agents:** Viper (near-mandatory; Toxic Screen halves the map), Jett (Operator platform from mid/A), Sova (wide Recon Bolt coverage), Killjoy (Nanoswarm A-site post-plant corner), Chamber (Tour De Force sniper)
- **Key Note:** Jett + Operator is "a different competitive category" on Breeze. Viper wall on B creates 2v5 defensive math.
- **Avoid:** Brimstone (Sky Smokes not wide enough), Phoenix (no long-range kit)

### FRACTURE
- **Layout:** Unique H-layout; attackers can attack both sites from opposite ends simultaneously. Rope runs through mid.
- **Attack/Defense:** Attack-sided due to pincer pressure; defenders cannot rotate both ways.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Brimstone + Neon + Breach + Cypher + Fade (56.5%)
- **Best Agents:** Breach (near-mandatory; stuns cover both site entry points), Raze (mobility + Showstopper rushes), Brimstone (Incendiary + Sky Smoke for double-site splits), Deadlock (Barrier Mesh blocks rotations), Fade (Haunt reveals defenders rotating)
- **Key Note:** Attackers hit sites from both sides at once. Flank coverage sentinel mandatory. Breach stuns are uniquely effective here.
- **Avoid:** Sova (few walls to bounce off), Jett (no clear Operator position)

### HAVEN
- **Layout:** Only 3-site map (A, B, C); long A site, small B, open C; extensive mid garage.
- **Attack/Defense:** Defense-sided due to 3-site spread.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Omen + Jett + Sova + Killjoy + Breach (56.8%)
- **Best Agents:** Sova (Recon Bolt covers A, C, and mid; Hunter's Fury cross-map), Killjoy (solo-holds a site; Lockdown rotations), Skye (wide-scouting Trailblazer for garage), Raze (C-site entry), Clove (smokes post-death hold the third site)
- **Key Note:** Three sites "stretch defenses thin." Wide-scouting initiator essential. Best triple-site defense setup = Killjoy + information agent.
- **Avoid:** Viper (wall not impactful across 3 sites), Iso (no escape from 3 angles of pressure)

### LOTUS
- **Layout:** 3 sites (A, B, C) with rotating silent doors; unique one-way drop mechanic.
- **Attack/Defense:** Balanced; rotating doors create unique rotation timing windows.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Omen + Raze + Fade + Killjoy + Gekko (56.8%)
- **Best Agents:** Killjoy (three separate site lock options), Skye/Fade (info for rotating-door timing), Raze (mobility between sites), Viper/Clove (smoke coverage for door areas), Omen (cross-map Dark Cover)
- **Key Note:** Silent doors mean rotations are stealthy; info agents critical to track. Post-plant through door = Nanoswarm optimal.
- **Avoid:** KAY/O (ZERO/point suppression wasted on maps with natural movement), Cypher (one camera per round not enough for 3 sites)

### PEARL
- **Layout:** No teleporters; heavily contested mid (Connector / Art / B-Link); two long-range sites.
- **Attack/Defense:** Attack-sided; mid control converts to site pressure.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Viper + Jett + Fade + Killjoy + KAY/O (56.5%)
- **Best Agents:** Fade (Haunt mid + Prowler A connector), Viper (Toxic Screen mid cut), Killjoy (B-site Nanoswarm), Jett (Operator mid or A-long), Cypher (B-link camera; specialty map for Cypher)
- **Key Note:** Fiercely contested mid with no teleporters = predictable rotations. Info + mid-control wins. Cypher's Spycam at B-link is elite here.
- **Avoid:** Breach (few walls for flashpoints in mid), Sage (slow orb not impactful enough on wide sites)

### SPLIT
- **Layout:** Vertical rope map; two mid towers; tight corridors; A ramps/screens, B mid/vents.
- **Attack/Defense:** Defense-sided; narrow entry points punish aggressive pushes.
- **Top Comp (by WR):** Omen + Raze + Breach + Cypher + Sage (56.8%)
- **Best Agents:** Viper (Toxic Screen at ramps blocks A entry), Omen (Dark Cover heights control), Killjoy (B-site anchor), Skye/Breach (flash initiators for safe rope/mid push), Raze (mobility up ramps, B mid)
- **Key Note:** "Vertical control is everything." Height advantage at A-screens and B-heaven define rounds. Sage Barrier Orb blocks ramp rush.
- **Avoid:** Sova (few flat surfaces for Recon Bolt; not a recon-heavy map), Yoru (decoys less useful in tight corridors)
