# Valorant — Bind Map Reference

**Status:** Current as of June 2026  
**Pool status:** NOT in current competitive rotation (removed Patch 12.08, April 2026 — replaced by Ascent). Active in Unrated, Swiftplay, Spike Rush, Escalation.  
**Defining feature:** Only Valorant map with **no mid lane** — replaced by **two one-way teleporters** connecting opposite sides of the map  
**Setting:** Radianite refinery off the coast of **Rabat, Morocco** (Alpha Earth). Kingdom Corporation facility built over historic Moroccan architecture — hookah lounges, ornate tilework, arched stonework, fountain plaza, water channel from the Bou Regreg River  
**Bomb sites:** A Site (Reactor Site A) and B Site (Reactor Site B)

---

## Map Overview

Bind has two bomb sites connected by two one-way teleporters instead of a traditional mid lane. Attackers enter from a single spawn and choose between A (via A Short or A Bath/Showers) and B (via B Long or B Hookah/Window). The teleporters allow rapid cross-map rotations but produce a **globally audible sound cue** when used, telegraphing movement.

**Teleporter summary:**
- **A Short TP → B Short (Hookah exit):** entrance at A Short near Lamps/U-Hall; exits players into the Hookah room above B site
- **B Long TP → A Lobby (Showers exit):** entrance at the end of B Long; exits players into A Lobby near A Bath/Showers

## Teleporter Mechanics (Critical for Comms)

| Mechanic | Detail |
|---|---|
| A Short TP (→ Hookah) | Entrance: at A Short / Lamps area. Exit: inside Hookah above B site. Travel time: ~1 second. |
| B Long TP (→ A Lobby) | Entrance: at the end of B Long (near the B fountain / orb). Exit: outside A Bath, near A Lobby. Travel time: ~1 second. |
| Audio cue | **Globally audible** — the entire lobby hears the TP sound when used. Signals rotation to both teams. |
| One-way | Cannot travel the reverse direction. Entering from the wrong side exits you normally (no TP). |
| Exit room | Players land in a small room and must walk out the exit door — brief window of vulnerability at exit. |
| Common tactic | Attackers fake A Short TP to bait defender rotation, or use B Long TP to rapidly reinforce A while simultaneously pressuring A Bath |

## Strategic Notes for Relay Corpus

- **No mid = teleporter is the "mid"** — controlling teleporter timing information (listening for TP sounds) replaces mid-game intel on every other map
- **TP audio is global** — when a teammate or enemy uses either TP, both teams hear it; always call out TP usage in comms ("TP A Short," "TP B Long")
- **A Bath (Showers) is the map's hardest entry** — tight corridor, many corners, standard defender utility dump; utility-heavy pushes are required
- **B Hookah is the B-site equivalent** — one-way window, elevated positioning, defenders commonly hold from under-hookah or back-site; smoke the window before pushing
- **A Lamps / U-Hall controls both entries** — a defender holding Lamps can see A Short approach and react to Bath; clear it before executing A
- **A Heaven** is mandatory to contest — the elevated platform gives free information on both Bath exit and A Short; losing it without a counter-smoke is costly
- **B Long TP to A Lobby** — a single player can take this TP late in the buy phase to appear at A Lobby while team pressures B; creates split timing without a traditional mid
- **TP baits** — faking a TP (walking into the entrance then backing out) will NOT trigger the sound; only completing the TP triggers global audio; can be used to bait defender rotations
- **B Garden / Octagon** — often used as a staging area before pushing B Short/Hookah; Sova Recon through here reveals Hookah and B site
- **Elbow hold** — the B Elbow position between B site and B Hall is a defender lurk that covers both the Hookah window and back-site; notoriously hard to clear
- **Shiny / Container wallbang** — the Shiny radianite crates at B site can be wall-banged; this is a known post-plant spot the relay must treat as a contact risk
- **Triple Box / Tetris** — the A site box stack is the primary spike plant location; smoke Heaven and clear Lamps before planting here
- **Post-plant on A** — Heaven-hold post-plant is extremely strong; forces attackers to smoke Heaven and fight up the stairs under time pressure
- **Sage Wall in Hookah** — Sage can wall the Hookah window to cut off one B site entry; common defensive setup
