# MAP: ABYSS

**Map type:** Two-site, three-lane, relatively balanced (slight attacker lean due to mid control leverage)  
**Setting:** Jan Mayen, Arctic Norway — the "Elder Grotto," a hidden Scions of Hourglass fortress buried under the island, seized by the VALORANT Protocol. Ancient artifacts and millennia of secrets.  
**Release date:** June 11, 2024 (Patch 8.11)  
**Signature mechanic:** NO OUTER BOUNDARIES — first VALORANT map where players can fall off edges and die. Multiple open drops around mid and both sites. Also features rope ascenders (like Split) for vertical traversal.  
**Current competitive pool:** NO (out since V26 Act 2, March 2026)  
**Map layout:** Two-site, three main lanes (A lane, Mid, B lane). Mid is split by a large void/death drop into two sub-lanes (Catwalk upper, Bottom lower).

```
[Attacker Spawn]
       |
   A Lobby -------- Mid Bottom -------- B Main
       |                 |                  |
   A Main          Mid Library          B Lobby
       |                 |

   / | \                 |
A Bridge  A Tower     A Vent (breakable door) → A Tower
A Back    A Secret
   |
[Defender Spawn] -- B Tower -- B Link -- B Site
                                          |  \
                                       B Nest  B Danger
```

**Rope ascenders (4 total):**
1. A Site → A Tower (from site floor up to tower level)
2. Attacker Spawn area → elevated platform
3. B Main → Mid Bend (from B Main side up into mid)
4. Mid Catwalk → access point (connects lower mid to catwalk elevation)

**Death-drop locations:**
- Center of Mid (the void between Mid Catwalk and Mid Bottom) — fall = instant death
- A Bridge edges — narrow walkway over a drop
- A Secret — floating outer ledge behind A Site, accessible from defender side
- B Danger — outer ledge behind B Site, used as a jump shortcut; fall = death

## ABYSS — UNIQUE MECHANICS SUMMARY

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| No outer boundaries | Only VALORANT map with no guardrails. Falling off any unguarded edge = instant death (same as fall damage death). |
| Rope ascenders | 4 total. Silent to use; audible if you're nearby. You are vulnerable (exposed animation) while ascending. |
| Breakable vent doors | 120 HP each. Color-coded health (blue → yellow → red). Two doors (one at each end of A Vent). Bullets + splash damage break them. |
| Death-drop void (Mid) | The central chasm in Mid separates Mid Top/Catwalk from Mid Bottom. Falling in = death. |
| A Bridge / B Danger ledges | Outer ledges with open drops. Useful tactical shortcuts but high fall risk. |

## ABYSS — AGENT PICKS (competitive consensus)

**Strong on Abyss:**
- **Jett / Neon** (duelists): No-boundary edges enable dash/slide-off plays; aggressive entry into narrow A Main / B Main
- **Killjoy** (sentinel): Lockdown + turret on B Site or A Tower holds are powerful
- **Sova** (initiator): Recon bolt lineups for A Main and B Main corners; shock dart on A Bridge plant
- **Omen / Brimstone** (controllers): Smokes for A Main cross and B Link; blind corners on both sites
- **Skye / Fade** (initiators): Flash for A Main push; info-gathering over mid void

**Avoid / weaker:** Harbor, Deadlock (less wall-exploit opportunity on these open lanes)

# MAP: CORRODE

**Map type:** Two-site, three-lane, defender-sided (especially B Site)  
**Setting:** Mont-Saint-Michel, Normandy, France — Omega Earth. An ancient French castle town repurposed as a radianite salt mining facility. After First Light caused catastrophic climate change, the waters around the island evaporated, exposing vast salt flats. Locals now extract and process them commercially.  
**Release date:** June 25, 2025 (Patch 11.00)  
**Signature mechanic:** FLOODED WALKWAYS — certain floor sections are submerged in shallow water. Running/rushing through them creates audible splashing footstep sounds that can reveal your position to nearby enemies. Must walk slowly to minimize noise. No other dynamic mechanical elements (static map — no teleporters, no doors, no ascenders).  
**Current competitive pool:** NO (out since V26 Act 2, March 2026 — rotated in for Act 1 only)  
**Map layout:** Two-site, three-lane. Mid is long and linear with clear elevation levels (Bottom → Stairs → Top). A site is the larger site with multiple angles. B site is smaller, more defender-favored with elevated tower positions.

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Flooded walkways | Shallow water in select floor sections. Running/rushing through water = loud audible splashing. Walk slowly to suppress sound. Locations are distributed through corridors/transitions throughout the map. |
| No teleporters | Unlike Bind; no instant repositioning tools. |

| No breakable elements | Pure static architecture. No doors, no walls to break. |

## CORRODE — SITE ATTACK/DEFENSE NOTES

**A Site:**
- Larger site; two entry paths (Yard and Elbow)
- Best attack: split A Main + Mid → take Yard from both Link and Main simultaneously
- Crane defender must be cleared/smoked for safe plant
- A Pocket must be cleared; angles from Pocket cover site after entry

**B Site:**
- Smaller, more defender-sided
- Features layered elevation: Tower, shed top, boxes, below-ramp position
- Best attack: split through Mid but needs utility commitment — smoke Tower and Elbow before committing
- "Draw out defensive utility before committing" — difficult even with Mid control
- B Arch defender position is strong; must be dislodged

**Mid:**
- "Mid first, then decide" — controlling Stairs/Top allows safe pivots to either site
- Long linear corridor; clear dueling space
- Mid Window provides key cross-map sight lines

## CORRODE — AGENT PICKS (competitive consensus)

**Strong on Corrode:**
- **Sova / Fade** (initiators): Thin walls throughout make Recon Bolt and Fade Haunt especially powerful for info
- **Vyse** (sentinel): Wall abilities perfectly lock down key positions; rated top sentinel for Corrode
- **Jett / Iso** (duelists): Elbow angles and tight B corridors reward precise gunfighters
- **Brimstone / Omen** (controllers): Essential for smoking Tower on B entry and Crane on A entry
- **Skye / Breach** (initiators): Flash utility to force through B's layered elevation angles

**Context:** B site's heavy defender bias makes initiation utility essential for attack-side round wins.
