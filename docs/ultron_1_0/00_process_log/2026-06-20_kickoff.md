### Architecture vision
1. **Route ALL responses through the LLM (8B).** Keep deterministic snapping paths, but
   repurpose them to *route* and *assign the correct curated prompt* to the LLM, and to
   *inject the relevant snap responses as in-context exemplars* to guide the LLM's reply.
2. **Optional wakeword / always-listening.** Wakeword still works but is optional. Ultron is
   always listening + transcribing, and uses advanced semantic matching + similarity scoring +
   fuzzy/better matching to detect when speech contains a command meant to relay to the team.
   Leverage the snap-callout library + normalization L1/L2 + semantics/routing as matchers.
   Must also catch non-explicit relay intent (like today's "word for word" / "say to my team").
   Must distinguish three scenarios:
   - (A) I'm speaking to someone else (e.g. a Discord call).
   - (B) I'm talking out loud / to my stream chat.
   - (C) I'm talking to Ultron but want a response to ME only, not the team.
3. **Flavor as verbosity:** change flavor on/off → **no / low / high flavor** controlling reply
   verbosity & length. ALSO keep a separate flavor on/off (whether a flavor tail is appended).
   Prompt construction tells the LLM the target verbosity based on the routed command (e.g.
   "sova hit 84" → minimal verbosity), and instructs the LLM to author its OWN custom flavor
   tail fitting the callout context.
4. **Keep agent-specific info/context/vocabulary/tail libraries** and inject them based on which
   agents appear / who is being addressed (specific person/agent or whole team), as snap routing
   does today.
5. **Keep aggregate libraries + ingestion/aggregation pipelines** so lines can be added easily.
6. **Do NOT remove current functionality** — retire & switch off, revisitable later.
7. **Strict Ultron persona** across all flavor libraries and prompts. Keep the personality.
